About Sargam Charitable Foundation

💡

Our Vision

To evolve a holistic support system that goes beyond treating illness — one that actively nurtures physical and mental wellness, upholds the dignity of the elderly, and builds inclusive community care for every person who is ailing, vulnerable, or simply in need of a hand.

🎯

Our Mission

We bridge the gap between need and help by funding critical treatments, supporting family caregivers, running preventive health programs, and making mental health conversations normal. Our work is grounded in communities, guided by compassion, and measured by real lives changed.

Our Programs

Four pillars of community care — each designed to touch lives where it matters most.

01
Medical Aid

Where Life-Saving Treatment Shouldn't Depend on Your Bank Balance

For families caught in the relentless cycle of chronic illness, the financial burden is often as devastating as the disease itself. Our Medical Aid program steps in to bridge that gap — ensuring that dialysis sessions continue, chemotherapy courses are completed and no one goes without care simply because they cannot pay.

🏥 Services
  • Renal Care — Long-term financial support for patients requiring regular dialysis.
  • Oncology — Covering chemotherapy and radiation costs for cancer patients.
  • Mobility Support — Assistive equipment and financial aid for the physically handicapped and accident victims.
⚙️ How We Operate
  • Hospital Partnerships — Tie-ups with government hospitals and charitable clinics for vetted patient referrals.
  • Direct-to-Hospital Payments — We pay service providers directly, bypassing the risk of fund misuse.
  • Verification Committee — A dedicated review body assessing applicants' financial status.
  • Medical Equipment Bank — Lending wheelchairs, oxygen concentrators and hospital beds for home-based care.
  • Consumables Drive — Monthly distribution of high-cost medical disposables.
"No one should have to choose between treatment and survival."
02
Elderly Care

Because Growing Older Should Mean Being Cared For, Not Forgotten

Our Elderly Care program extends beyond medical support. It's about restoring dignity, independence and joy in the lives of senior citizens. We empower families with limited resources to care for their elders with compassion, equip communities with the means to support their elders and ensure that every elderly individual feels seen, valued and included.

🎯 Focus Areas
  • Nutrition — Monthly ration kits and supplements for the feeble and homebound elderly.
  • Hygiene & Dignity — Adult care essentials to maintain cleanliness, comfort and self-respect.
  • Caregiver Support — Training, guidance and financial relief for family caregivers.
  • Social Inclusion — Events and campaigns to reduce isolation and foster community connection.
⚙️ How We Operate
  • Silver Support Kits — Monthly care packages for elderly citizens.
  • Caregiver Skills Training — Workshops including bed patient care and bedsore prevention.
  • Respite Grants — Financial support for family members who care for bedridden elders.
  • Home Care Guide — Step-by-step guidance materials for elder care at home.
  • Community Events — Social gatherings and engaging programs for senior citizens.
  • Youth Awareness Drives — Campaigns encouraging younger generations to care for elders.
"Every elder deserves to age with grace and dignity."
03
Physical Wellness

Shifting the Conversation from Cure to Prevention

The best healthcare is the kind you never need. Our Physical Wellness program brings preventive health education directly into communities equipping people with the knowledge, screenings, and habits to stay ahead of lifestyle diseases before they take hold.

🎯 Focus Areas
  • Metabolic Health — Diabetes and NAFLD awareness.
  • Cardiovascular Health — Hypertension and cholesterol management.
  • Dietary Reform — Promoting healthier food habits.
⚙️ Activities
  • 'Know Your Numbers' Camps — Community health camps with BP, sugar, and BMI checks.
  • 'Eat Right' Workshops — Live healthy cooking demonstrations.
  • Digital Health Series — Weekly social media awareness campaigns.
  • Health Literacy Materials — Educational materials on lifestyle diseases.
"Prevention is not a luxury. It is the most powerful medicine."
04
Mental Wellness

Making It Safe to Ask for Help

Mental health struggles don't announce themselves and for too many, silence feels like the only option. Our Mental Wellness program works to change that, creating spaces where students can breathe, professionals can seek balance and families can have honest conversations about emotional well-being.

👥 Focus Groups
  • Students — Stress, anxiety, and academic pressure.
  • Working Professionals — Burnout, work-life balance and workplace stress.
  • Families — Parenting stress, relationship dynamics and emotional well-being.
⚙️ Activities
  • School & College Sessions — Stress management sessions for students.
  • Workplace Wellness Talks — Sessions on burnout and maintaining work-life balance.
  • Mental Health Content — Short contents and explainer videos on mental health in simple language.
  • Helpline Coordination — Connecting individuals with crisis helplines to offer safe, anonymous access to immediate professional support.
"You don't have to be in crisis to deserve support."
